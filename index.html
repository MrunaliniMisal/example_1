IsquareIT Society for Electronics and Telecommunication Students Welcomes You!

Hello and greetings to you all. ISETS stands for IsquareIT Society for Electronics and Telecommunication Students. The purpose of this association is to promote fellowship and unity within the Electronics and Telecommunication students on campus. Each and every ENTC student is a member of this association. We aim towards the betterment of every student in both technical and non-technical aspects. Each member is invited to become a part of every event and activity we conduct. Our association is divided into three subgroups viz. Technical, Social and Personality Development. We conduct various activities and events under these.


ISETS has conducted many events in the past like workshops, seminars and industrial visits to name a few. Our social wing has also undertaken the task of celebrating festivals on campus for a better cause. We also plan to conduct quiz competitions, games and also many more sessions for our members. Our committee is striving to achieve the aim of all-round development of our students and we have to thank all the faculty members who are also giving us a helping hand in this task.
